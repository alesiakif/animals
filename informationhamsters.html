A hamster is a small furry animal that is often kept as a pet. Hamsters usually have a round body and short legs and their fur can be brown, black, white or a combination of several colours.

Hamsters like to eat, especially foods such as seeds and grains. They also like to store food in their cheeks. They are very sociable animals and love to play with different objects.

Hamsters are very easy to look after. They need a cage with plenty of space to move around and enough bedding to burrow and hide in.

Hamsters are a great pet for children as they require little care and can be very loving. They are also very adaptable and can live in both warm and cool environments.

Hamsters are wonderful pets, a little love and care and your hamster will live a long and healthy life.
