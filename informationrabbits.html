Rabbits are cute furry animals that can make great pets. They have long ears, short tails, and four long legs. Rabbits come in different colors like brown, white, gray, and black.

Rabbits are herbivores, which means they eat plants. They feed on grasses, clover, and other leafy greens. They also eat fruits and vegetables. Rabbits also like to eat root vegetables, such as carrots and potatoes.

Rabbits like to eat hay, vegetables, and fruits. Rabbits are very social animals and like to be around other animals and people.

Rabbits can be litter trained just like cats and can be very friendly and cuddly. They love to be petted and scratched behind the ears. Rabbits are very quiet so they make good pets for people who live in apartments.

Rabbits are very active animals and need lots of exercise. They can jump and run around, and even do tricks like going through tunnels and jumping over obstacles.

Rabbits are absolutely adorable and make wonderful pets.
