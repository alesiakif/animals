The dog is a pet animal. It has four legs, two ears, two eyes and a short tail. Dogs are of many types, different colours and size. Dogs don’t only see in black and white — they can also see blue and yellow.

It is found everywhere. It has a sharp sense of hearing and smell. The dog is a very useful and faithful animal. It loves its master very much. It guards the house from thieves with care. It is right to call him man’s best friend.
